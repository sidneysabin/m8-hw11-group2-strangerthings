Meet the Cast

The Party

Caleb McLaughlin

Lucas Sinclair

Always questioning the abnormal, including Eleven. Logical, yet sweet-natured with a good heart. Deeply in love with Max. The most athletic out of The Party, great with a basketball and a slingshot.

Finn Wolfhard

Mike Wheeler

The Leader of the Party and member of the Hellfire Club. Dungeon Master extraordinaire. Strategic thinker with natural leadership and loyalty. Boyfriend to Eleven, brother to Nancy. Always has a walkie talkie on hand.

Gaten Matarazzo

Dustin Henderson

Observer and a protector of the unity within the Party. Plagued by high intelligence and wisdom, always exploring the unnatural and getting into trouble. Met his girlfriend at code camp. Loves to sing "Never Ending Story."

Noah Schnapp

Will Byers

The Mind Flayer's first victim. Spent an entire season in The Upside Down. Soft-spoken with a passion for creativity and art. Always putting others before himself. Known as the "Zombie Boy" who can communicate with Christmas lights.

Hopkins Middle School

Milie Bobby Brown

Eleven

Escaped from Hawkins National Laboratory, being hunted by Dr. Brenner. Throwing vans into space. Medium between Hawkins and the Upside Down. Loves Eggo waffles and Mike. Bad news when you see her bloody nose.

Sadie Sink

Max Mayfield

Originally from California, a new addition to The Party in Season 2. Loves skateboarding. Lost her stepbrother Billy to the Mind Flayer in Season 3, and became one of Vecna's victims in Season 4. Favorite song is "Running up that Hill" by Kate Bash. Known as "MADMAX" at Palace Arcade.

Priah Ferguson

Erica Sinclair

The younger sister of Lucas. Always harassing Steve and Robin at Scoops Ahoy for free samples. Intelligent and brave with a passion for math and My Little Pony. A nerd for Dungeons and Dragons, became a member of The Hellfire Club in Season 4.

Hopkins High School

Charles Eaton

Jonathan Byers

Small town boy with a big heart. Smart, introverted, asocial, kind, independent, and brave. First son of Joyce and brother to Will. Excellent photographer and journalist with a skill for detective work. Always solving mysteries and getting into trouble with his girlfriend, Nancy.

Natalia Dyer

Nancy Wheeler

Mike's older sister and Jonathan's boyfriend. She also once dated Steve. Extremely intelligent with bravery and resolve. Passionate about journalism and solving mysteries, even if it means getting in trouble. Still thinks about Barb.

Joe Keery

Steve Harrington

The former dream boy of Hawkins High School. Once dated Nancy and enemies with Jonathan, but now a faithful ally and best friends with Dustin. Snuck into a Soviet base under Starcourt Mall. Hands out free samples at Scoops Ahoy and skilled with a spiked baseball bat.

Maya Hawke

Robin Buckley

Worked at Scoops Ahoy with Steve and helped break into the underground Soviet base. The most sarcastic out of the group but overly compassionate for her friends.

Joseph Quinn

Eddie Munson

The Dungeon Master of the Hellfire Club. Full-on nonconformist metalhead who is extremely kind, open-minded, and comforting. Blamed for bad things happening in Hawkins, when it's really Vecna's fault. Best friends with Dustin and top-notch electric guitarist.

The Adults

Winona Ryder

Joyce Byers

Single mother of Jonathan and Will. Brave, amicable, and benevolent. Always kept faith when Will went missing. High school crush of Chief Hopper. Used Christmas lights to communicate with Will in the Upside Down and broke Hopper out of a Russian prison.

David Harbour

Jim Hopper

Police Chief of Hawkins. Lost his first daughter to cancer. Despite his laid back attitude as Chief, will do anything to protect his new daughter, Eleven. Sacrificed himself to save Hawkins and ended up in Soviet prison. Expert combatant, survivalist, and craftsman.

Brett Gellman

Murray Bauman

Former reporter for The Chicago Sun-Times. Always ignored by others due to his eccentric behavior and strange reports in solving investigations. Incredibly nonchalant and relaxed. Black belt in Karate who is talented with a double-barrel shotgun.
